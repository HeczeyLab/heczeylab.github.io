Meet our team!

Andras Heczey, MD

Principal Investigator

Dr. Heczey is a pediatric oncologist and investigator at Seattle Children's Research Institute. His work focuses on developing innovative CAR T-cell therapies for pediatric and adult patients.

He leads the Heczey Lab in translating basic scientific discoveries into life-saving clinical trials.
