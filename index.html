Our Mission

The Heczey Lab is dedicated to advancing the field of cancer immunotherapy by engineering next-generation CAR T cells. Our goal is to translate basic scientific discoveries into life-saving clinical trials for pediatric and adult patients.

Commitment to Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion

We believe that scientific excellence is cultivated in a diverse and inclusive environment. The Heczey Lab is committed to fostering a culture where researchers from all backgrounds, regardless of race, gender, orientation, or disability, are empowered to bring their unique perspectives to the fight against cancer. We strive to dismantle barriers in STEM and develop the next generation of scientific leaders . We are proud to work for an organization that maintains a commitment to workforce diversity.
